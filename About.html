About

In-house manufacturing and servicing is not only good for our customers; it’s environmentally responsible as well. We are able to dramatically reduce manufacturing waste throughout the entire process. Foam pieces are molded to exact size rather than excessive trimming, eliminating waste. Metal components are laser cut which also minimizes waste. Scrap metal is collected and recycled as well.
